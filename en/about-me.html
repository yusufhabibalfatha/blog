About Me

“Nothing is impossible with computers.”

I came across this quote when I successfully completed several simple website projects. Although the projects were relatively simple, the required features felt quite complex for me at the time due to my limited experience.

Early Journey

I started learning web development in 2020 while studying at Brawijaya University, majoring in Information Systems. At that time, I was in my fifth semester and still a complete beginner. I only understood the basics such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Laravel.

Project Experience

After graduating, I began taking on small projects, mainly helping events run more smoothly with the support of web technology.

Some of these included real-time attendance systems using barcodes, automatic reporting to Google Spreadsheets, and integration with WhatsApp for communication needs.

From these experiences, I realized one important thing: building a website is not about complicated technology, but about solving problems in a simple and effective way.

How I See Technology

From my experience, I believe that with technology—especially websites—almost any problem can be addressed with the right solution.

With today’s advancements, the presence of AI can help speed up the process. However, what matters most is how we design problem-solving frameworks, test them with real users, and continuously gather feedback.

Closing

This is a small page that tells a little about me. Most of my experiences and thoughts will be shared through articles on this blog.

I also want to continue improving my writing and reading skills, and I hope to receive feedback from every piece I publish.
